While There's Tea There's Hope

Below you'll find a list of all of the wonderful teas Teacloud has to offer. Our teas are grouped by type to make your selections easier.

Black Teas

English Breakfast (19.95/lb.)
A traditional tea from Ceylon, broken orange pekoe.

Earl Grey (14.95/lb.)
This Earl Grey is aromatic and stimulating. The blend starts with Ceylon black leaves and includes lemon peel, cornflower blossoms, jasmine flowers, lemongrass and just the right touch of Bergamot oil to make the distinction of this ever classic pleaser.

Darjeeling (18.95/lb.)
This exquisite Darjeeling -- or Sungma Muscatel -- is strong and very aromatic. It comes directly from the Singalila range below the Sundakphu ridge in India, and enjoyed by royalty throughout the tea-drinking world.

Almond Tea (18.95/lb.)
A common flavored form of black tea, remarkably fragrant.

Vanilla Bean (15.95/lb.)
Madagascar Vanilla beans and caramel, blended with Ceylon and China black teas. An uplifting fruity tea, promoting a sense of well being. Great iced.

Green Teas

Jasmine Tea (16.95/lb.)
The best know of the "scented" variations on green tea. Scented teas are made by blending flowers and petals with oolong and green teas. Jasmine, a mix of green tea and fresh jasmine flowers, is considered to have health benefits superior to straight green teas.

Orange Jasmine Blossom (18.95/lb.)
Orange slices and jasmine blossoms blended with Ceylon and China black teas. A spirited and soothing, citrus and floral blend.

China Dragon Well (22.95/lb.)
Lung Ching or Dragonwell is one of the most famous Chinese green teas. There are several grades of Dragon Well produced, of which this is the finest. Pale emerald green liquor, complex vegetal/mineral aroma; a superb tea.

Oolong Teas

Fine Formosa Tea (26.95/lb.)
Formosa is the former name of Taiwan, and is the source of the most sought after of the oolong teas. Sturdy enough to last through multiple infusions.

Jade Oolong (18.95/lb.)
This oolong tea from the mountainous region of Nantou, Taiwan, has a fresh flavor, smooth and light body with a flowery aroma cherished by oolong tea lovers across the globe. This type of oolong tea is known in the West as green oolong and has nuances of green and oolong tea.

Peppermint (15.95/lb.)
This peppermint tea is as good as it comes; a wonderful palate refresher, good anytime.

London Black Tea (14.95/lb.)
A blend of 3 bold black teas from Ceylon, Darjeeling and Assam. Jump start your morning with our version of an English Breakfast Blend.

Organic Darjeeling (25.95/lb.)
We have selected superior, single estate teas, from the Darjeeling region of India, which yield a subtle fruity and sweet, highly aromatic medium cup with a full body finish. This selection will vary from year to year.

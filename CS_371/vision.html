the Employee Accountability Review System

 

This system helps to keep employees accountable for their actions and behavior.  Corporate greed and employee dishonesty and incompetence has been the root of many recent problems across many industries. This system will help hiring managers and others to easily identify excellent poor employees.

 

1. Corporate incompetence robs society.

 

2. The public receives fewer social services due to corporate tax evasion. Shareholders of corrupt corporations are abused.

 

3. These problems open a new door of opportunity for us. We have created a system that will allow for employees to rate each other’s performance / behavior in the workplace.

 

The system is for those:
-Interested in reviewing their fellow employee’s performance anonymously.
-Curious about the opinions of their fellow employee’s former co-workers on their performance.
-Interested in reviewing their place of employment anonymously.
-Looking for a new job and interested in the opinions of former employee’s that worked there.

 

  1. Employees may use the system to search, inspect, review, and rate their colleagues.
  2. Hiring Managers will use the system to review job applicants

 

This product provides a way to gather information about corporations and their employees in a way that is far more accessible than anything currently in existence.  By allowing anyone to anonymously post a large amount of reviews can be collected over a short period of time.  This provides a unique opportunity for users to gain information about work environments before actually working there.  Likewise it allows employers to get a general idea of how potential employee’s previous co-workers feel about their performance.

 

This system:

  • Allows anyone to easy access to write reviews and give opinions on their fellow employees.
  • Provides the opportunity to see the opinions of former employees on prospective work place.
  • Allows users to anonymously review their work place.
  • Gives employers easy access to peer reviews of prospective employees.
  • Helps hiring managers get a feel for the work ethic and attitude of job applicants.
  • Increases Employee Accountability
